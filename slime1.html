About Us

Slime and Johnny Solar Solutions is a solar energy company based in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State. We specialize in delivering high-quality solar installations and consultancy services to homeowners, estates, and NGOs.

Our mission is simple: to empower communities with clean, reliable energy while ensuring every customer feels heard, supported, and satisfied.
